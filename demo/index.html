CaseProof AI

A small gate for Maestro cases. It asks whether an agent-handled case has enough evidence, policy fit, and stage history to be sent to a person.

Scenario 1: HOLD

Evidence is missing

The case may be valid, but it is not review-ready.

HOLD
  • required_evidence_missing Required evidence kind is missing: customer_statement
  • required_evidence_missing Required evidence kind is missing: agent_rationale
  • rationale_ref_missing Decision rationale ref is not in evidence packet: EV-rationale

Scenario 2: REVIEW

Ready for a person

The agent can open a human review task. It cannot approve the case.

ALLOW_HUMAN_REVIEW_ONLY
  • No blocking findings. Human review remains required.

Scenario 3: BLOCK

Policy is bypassed

The agent tries to auto-approve a case that requires review.

BLOCK
  • external_action_enabled External business action is enabled before human approval
  • amount_requires_human_review Amount exceeds auto-action cap without human review route
  • human_review_route_missing Policy requires human review for this amount
  • blocked_override_reason Decision uses a blocked policy override reason

Scenario 4: BLOCK

A Maestro stage was skipped

The case jumped from evidence collection to recommendation.

BLOCK
  • stage_order_invalid Required Maestro case stages are missing or out of order
External business actions stay disabled. The only allowed action is human review.
