# Claim Ceiling

CaseProof AI is a public-safe hackathon prototype.

It demonstrates a local validator over synthetic UiPath Maestro case packets.

It does not claim:

- production approval
- legal compliance
- financial authorization
- security certification
- real customer-data validation
- field readiness

Allowed claim:

```text
CaseProof AI can demonstrate, on synthetic packets, how a Maestro case can be held, blocked, or routed to human review.
```

Blocked claim:

```text
CaseProof AI is ready to approve real business cases.
```

Next validation:

Bind the same packet shape to a real UiPath Maestro case URL, process instance, evidence references, and human review task.
